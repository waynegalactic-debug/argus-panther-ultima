### Evidence Grading System

| Grade | Definition | Usage |
|-------|------------|-------|
| **VERIFIED** | Confirmed by official source | SEC filing, USPTO record, live API |
| **PROVEN** | Multiple independent confirmations | Cross-referenced across 2+ sources |
| **PARTIAL** | Some supporting evidence | Incomplete data or single source |
| **NO_EVIDENCE** | No data found despite search | Does not mean false, just unverified |
| **REFUTED** | Official sources contradict claim | Actively disproven |

### Restitution Modeling Framework

**Scenario Matrix (5 overlap scenarios):**
- S1A_Full: 100% overlap — all patents derived from victim
- S1B_Complete: 100% overlap — comprehensive infringement
- S1C_High: 70% overlap — high-confidence partial overlap
- S1D_Moderate: 50% overlap — moderate partial overlap
- S1E_Minimal: 25% overlap — minimal overlap

**Valuation Methods (5 approaches):**
- M2A_Cost: Cumulative R&D expenditure × overlap years
- M2B_Asset: Total IP asset book value
- M2C_MktCap: 8% of market capitalization
- M2D_Royalty: 3% of cumulative revenue
- M2E_PerPatent: $5M-$50M per patent × count

**Legal Multipliers:**
- Willful infringement: 1.8× (35 U.S.C. § 284)
- Attorney fees: 1.3× (prevailing party)
- Prejudgment interest: 4% compounded over 5 years = 1.217×
